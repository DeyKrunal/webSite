Enable "Less secure app access" in your Google Account:

Go to your Google Account.
https://myaccount.google.com/security
Navigate to the "Security" tab.
Scroll down to the "Less secure app access" section.
Turn on "Allow less secure apps."
Generate an App Password:

While still in the "Security" tab, find the "Signing in to Google" section.
Click on "App passwords."
Select "Mail" and "Other (Custom name)" in the app dropdown.
Click "Generate."
Copy the App Password:

A 16-character App Password will be generated. Copy this password; you will use it in your script.
